Rules & Regulations of the Youth Industrialization Movement Ghana

Welcome! Please read the rules and regulations carefully before proceeding.

  • Admission Criteria:

    All individuals seeking to participate in YIMG programs must meet the specified admmision criteria, including age requirements and educational qualifications.

  • Code of Conduct:

    Participants are expected to adhere to a code of conduct that promotes respect, professionalism, and ethical behaviour at all times.

  • Attendance:

    Regular attendance is mandatory for all training sessions, workshops, and other program activities. participants are required to notify program Coordinators in advance in case of unavoidable absences.

  • Safety and Security:

    YIMG prioritizes the safety and security of all participants. Measures are in place to ensure a safe learning environment including adherence ti health and safety protocols.

  • Use of Resources:

    Participants are responsible for the proper use and care of all resources provided by YIMG, including equipment, materials, and facilities.

  • Financial Management:

    Participants receiving financial support from YIMG must adhere to guidelines for the use and management of funds, including budgeting, record-keeping, and accountability.

  • Intellectual Property:

    Participants are expected to respect the intellectual property rights of others and adhere to copyright and trademark laws in the development and use of their work.

  • Collaboration and Network:

    Participants are encouraged to collaborate with fellow participants, mentors, and external partners to maximize learning opportunities and expand their networks.

  • Evaluation and Feedback:

    YIMG conducts regular evaluation to assess program effectiveness and gather feedback from participants. Participants are encouraged to provide constructive feedback to improve program delivery.

  • Compliance with Laws and Regulations:

    Participants must comply with all relevant laws, regulations and policies governing their activities, including those related to business registration, taxation and environmental protection.

  • Confidentiality:

    Participants are expected to maintain the Confidentiality of sensitive information shared during program activities including personal data and proprietary business information.

  • Non-Discrimination:

    YIMG is committed to promoting diversity, equity, and inclusion. Discrimination or harrassment based on race, gender, religion, ethnicity, disability or any other characteristics is strictly prohibited.

  • Conflict Resolution:

    In the event of disputes or conflicts among participants, YIMG provides mechanisms for peaceful resolution including mediation and arbitration.

  • Termination of Participation:

    YIMG reserves the right to terminate the participation of any individual who violates program rules and regulations or engages in behaviour deemed detrimental to the goals and values of the organization.

  • Amendement of Rules:

    These rules and regulations may be amended or updated by YIMG as necessary to ensure the effective operation of its program and activities. Participants will be notified of any changes in advance.
