Shoe Manufacturing

Through hands-on training, mentorship, and access to quality materials, we empower youth to create high-quality, locally-made footwear.

Garment Making

Our goal is to empower the youth to design and produce high-quality, locally-made clothing that meets both local and international standards.

Recycling and Waste Management

Through our Recycling and Waste Management initiative, we train and empower young Ghanaians to turn waste into valuable resources.

CCTV Installation and Security System

By fostering expertise in modern security solutions, we aim to create job opportunities while enhancing safety and security across Ghana.

Mobile phones Manufacturing

We empower young innovators with skills in hardware and software to reduce dependence on imports, create jobs, and position Ghana as a hub for technological innovation in Africa.

Detergents and Toiletries production

We equip youth to manufacture essential products such as soaps, disinfectants, shampoos, and liquid detergents.
